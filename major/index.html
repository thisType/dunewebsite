Directed by Denis Villeneuve, Dune is a 2021 science fiction epic set in a far-off future where noble houses rule planets. The story follows Paul Atreides, son of Duke Leto Atreides, as his family takes control of the desert planet Arrakis, the only source of spice, a vital resource. However, danger lurks as th e previous rulers, the cruel Harkonnens, plot to take back Arrakis. Betrayal and escape force Paul and his mother, Lady Jessica, into the harsh desert where they encounter the Fremen, the planet's native people. The film leaves us hanging as Paul begins to integrate with the Fremen, hinting at a rebellion and his potential role as their leader.
