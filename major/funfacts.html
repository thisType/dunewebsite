Plot

The Spice of life is the most valuable resource on Arrakis. This magical dust extends life, grants superhuman abilities to those who consume it in large quantities, and is essential for interstellar travel.

Giant Sandworm is a creature as long as a skyscraper burrowing through the desert sands. These formidable creatures are both a danger and a source of resources on Arrakis. People travel across the desert by navigating around them, and their bodies are harvested for valuable materials.

Living on a harsh desert planet requires some special adaptations known as Fremen Supersuit. The Fremen, the native population of Arrakis, wear stillsuits that recycle their sweat and body moisture. This allows them to survive in the desert's scorching heat and conserve precious water.
